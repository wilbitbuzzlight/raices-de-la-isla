Who We Are

Rooted in Culture,
Powered by Community

Raíces de la Isla Health Collective is a nurse-led 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to advancing health equity in underserved communities across New York City.

Founded on the belief that healthcare should be accessible, preventative, and rooted in community, Raíces works to bridge gaps in care through education, outreach, and direct services.

We are especially committed to serving communities with limited access to healthcare, including individuals experiencing homelessness and those disproportionately affected by chronic illness.

By partnering with local organizations, shelters, and leaders, we aim to create sustainable impact and build trust within the communities we serve.

"Raíces is rooted in culture, community, and compassion — committed to building partnerships and creating long-term solutions that uplift and support the health of every community we serve."

Health Education

Educating Our Communities

Accessible, culturally aware health education delivered directly to the people we serve.

Understanding Pneumonia: What You Need to Know

Causes, symptoms, prevention, and when to seek care — empowering communities to make informed health decisions.

Community Health Screenings

Free blood pressure screenings at community events, helping individuals identify potential health risks early.

Community Outreach & Health Fairs

Organizing and participating in health fairs and outreach initiatives, bringing resources and support directly to the people who need them.

Health Education

Education on chronic disease prevention, wellness, and healthy lifestyle practices — equipping communities with the tools to make informed decisions.

School-Based Education

Engaging students through educational sessions in schools, promoting early awareness of health and preventative care for long-term wellness.

Community Partnerships

Collaborating with local organizations, shelters, and community leaders to expand access to care and strengthen our collective impact.

Future Initiatives

Developing health kits with essential wellness supplies and expanding our services to reach more communities across New York City.
